Get to Know Your Mentor

During the first few weeks of the program, you are connected with a mentor with a similar personality who is familiar with your possible career path. You will be invited to several social events where mentors and mentees can get to know each other.

Complete Tasks and Training

You'll be provided with tasks and training material to guide your career path after meeting with your mentor. Once your mentor gets to know you personally and professionally, your tasks and training are tailored to you. During an agreed-upon time, you and your mentor will meet to discuss progress and any other needs. These meetings allow the two of you to catch up and adjust your tasks and training if necessary.

Graduation

At the end of the TLPD Program, you'll have a career plan laid out for you. The plan will give you step-by-step instructions on working confidently in your current career and advancing your career here at Northrop Grumman. We look forward to seeing you in the TLDP Program.
